## Notes on Sources

**Living Tradition**: Garuda is central to living Hindu and Buddhist religious practice and serves as national symbol for multiple nations. Sources should be approached with respect for both religious and national significance.

**National Symbol**: Special sensitivity required when discussing Garuda's role as national emblem of Indonesia, Thailand, and Mongolia.

**Regional Variations**: Buddhist and Hindu interpretations vary. Japanese Karura and Mongolian Khangarid represent distinct regional adaptations.
